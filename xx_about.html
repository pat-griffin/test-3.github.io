My love of architecture stems from the constant urge to explore the built environment and its immediate impact on our daily lives.  I am a firm believer that exploration leads to good design, and that architects must draw upon both past and present experiences to truly understand their work.  While my architectural interests are broad in the sense that I am fascinated by design at all scales, I am particularly interestested by the ways in which architecture can influence the future development and overall culture of our cities.  At Gensler, I hope to witness this level of impact first-hand while exploring the city of Denver and collaborating with my classmates to generate meaningful research into the design strategies and technologies that will guide our cities into the future.
